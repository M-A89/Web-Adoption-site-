Important Cat Care Tips:

All this is pasted from: Pet Safe

  1. Groom your cat regularly
    • This helps remove loose hair from the coat, preventing shedding and hairballs.
  2. Provide fresh water 24/7
    • Proper hydration can keep your cat comfortable and happy by helping to prevent common cat health issues like UTIs and kidney disease.
  3. Make sure your cat always has a place to potty
    • A general rule of thumb is to provide one for each cat in your home, plus one more.
  4. Keep the litter box clean
    • Many cats will find another place to go (like the carpet... or your pillow) if their litter box hasn't been cleaned in a while.
  5. Train your cat to use a scratching post
    • Regular scratching on an appropriate surface such as carpet, sisal, or cardboard helps remove the old layers from your cat's claws.
  6. Maintain a healthy diet and weight
    • To maintain a healthy diet, talk to your vet about which food is best for your cat. If your vet recommends dry food diet, consider an automatic pet feeder to help maintain portion control.
